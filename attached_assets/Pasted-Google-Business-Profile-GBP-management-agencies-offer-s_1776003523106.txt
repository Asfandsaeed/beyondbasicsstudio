## Tier 1: Basic ($200/month)
This entry-level tier focuses on foundational maintenance to ensure GBP compliance and visibility, ideal for single-location businesses with limited budgets.

- **Profile Monitoring & Audits**:
  - Daily automated scans for NAP (Name, Address, Phone) inconsistencies across 50+ directories.
  - Weekly manual audits checking business hours, holidays, services, attributes (e.g., "wheelchair accessible"), and labels.
  - Alerts for suspensions, policy violations, or competitor sabotage attempts.
  - Basic spam detection and reporting of fake reviews or unauthorized edits.

- **Core Optimizations**:
  - Primary and secondary category selection (up to 10 categories) with keyword research for relevance.
  - Initial upload of 20+ high-quality photos (exterior, interior, logo, cover) optimized for mobile.
  - Description writing (750 characters) incorporating local keywords and calls-to-action.
  - URL linking to website, menu, booking page, or social profiles.
  - Service area setup for non-physical locations (up to 20 areas).

- **Content & Engagement**:
  - 1-2 monthly Google Posts (updates, offers, events) with images and CTAs.
  - Basic Q&A seeding (5 common questions answered proactively).
  - Monthly insight checks (views, searches, actions) with simple PDF summary.

- **Support & Compliance**:
  - Email support (48-hour response).
  - Monthly compliance report (1-page PDF).
  - Ownership verification if needed (one-time).
